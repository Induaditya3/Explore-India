Most Popular Tours of India

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Experience the grandeur of Mughal architecture.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

The Pink City offers magnificent palaces, forts, and vibrant culture. Don't miss the Amber Fort and Hawa Mahal.

Kerala Backwaters

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala's backwaters on a traditional houseboat. Perfect for relaxation.

Goa Beaches

Golden sands, azure waters, and vibrant nightlife. Goa offers the perfect beach vacation experience.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

The spiritual capital of India. Experience the ancient rituals along the Ganges River.

Himalayan Trekking

Explore the majestic mountains of the Himalayas. Perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Ranthambore National Park

Spot the majestic Bengal tiger in its natural habitat. A paradise for wildlife enthusiasts.

Khajuraho Temples

Marvel at the intricate architecture and sculptures of these UNESCO World Heritage temples.

Weekend Plans

Himalayan Adventure

Day 1

Arrive in Manali, check into hotel, explore local markets and enjoy scenic views of the mountains.

Day 2

Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding and zorbing, then return to Delhi.

Goa Beach Getaway

Day 1

Arrive in Goa, relax at Baga Beach, enjoy water sports and visit local flea market.

Day 2

Explore Old Goa churches, visit Dudhsagar Falls, and enjoy the nightlife at Tito's Lane.

Mughal Heritage Tour

Day 1

Visit Agra Fort and Itimad-ud-Daulah (Baby Taj) with local guide for historical insights.

Day 2

Explore Taj Mahal at sunrise, enjoy a boat ride on Yamuna River, then return to Delhi.
